NOTE: Prefigurative Operations invites readers to dismantle and refigure texts within the adversarial constraints of colonial racial capitalist language, using critical counter-texts. Participants are invited to dismantle and refigure The designer only hopes to be a vector for a few spores of the counter-texts' meanings and forms, not mine to fully know. Due to the hegemonic dynamics of algorithmic language processing, all texts are considered only in their English translation. The other side of this coin is the greater subtlety, evasions, and obscurities from surveillance possible in languages that the Large Language Models and other Natural Language Processors aren't yet trained on (Spanish, French, or Portuguese cannot really be considered safe in this way).
